About

Hello. I’m Shrey. I make things.

I'm an interaction designer and web developer based in New Delhi, India passionate about solving problems. Currently aged 16, I am a highschool senior at The Mother's International School in New Delhi. I believe that good design is as little design as possible, and this is something I try to reflect in my work — clean, yet elegant; simple, yet efficient.

I like solving problems with code, and in my free time, I enjoy working on personal projects and experimenting with new tools and languages.
