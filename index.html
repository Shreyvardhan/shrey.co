Hey, I'm Shrey.

I make things.

Hailing from New Delhi, India, I'm currently a freshman at Harvard University, Class of 2022. Apart from being a full-time student, I'm an interaction designer and web developer based out of Cambridge, MA, passionate about solving problems. I believe that good design is as little design as possible, and this is something I try to reflect in my work — clean, yet elegant; simple, yet efficient.

I like solving problems with code, and in my free time, I enjoy working on personal projects and experimenting with new tools and languages.

Currently looking  for Summer 2019 Internships. Get in touch.

I craft minimal experiences.

I work with organisations, start-ups, schools, and individuals to craft honest, meaningful, and unobtrusive visual identities, with an emphasis on simplicity and clarity of vision.

I believe that purpose is fundamental to design, and much of my work involves using code and design to innovate elegant solutions to everyday problems, from combating garbage-strewn streets in Delhi to fighting for equal property rights for Indian women.
